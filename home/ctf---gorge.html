CTF - Gorge

CTF Gorge is a 5v5 player capture the flag map that I made for Unreal Tournament. It is set in a gorge with two facing caves, connected by an exposed bridge formation in the middle.

Level Overview

Design Goal

The map is straight forward and easy to learn but is designed for a high-tempo experience where the players are always on their toes, either jumping across the map, engaging in battle or doing both. Its design has an emphasis on teamplay, base defence and assault, with the centre of conflict right outside each base. Its curvy paths make moving across the map organic and flowy.

Detailed Experience

As you spawn, you must make a choice between attack or defence. Choosing to attack, means throwing yourself straight into combat as you must pass over the exposed crossing that separates the bases. The openness of the upper crossing encourages long range combat and passing over it could be difficult, unless you work together as a team and provide covering fire.

The lower levels of the crossing acts as a safer way across, with plenty of cover from sniper angles. Its tight corners and narrow pathways makes for a high-tension experience as there could be an enemy around every corner. There are many paths and slopes that connect the upper and lower level, so that you never have to feel stuck.

In the flag room, you will have to move fast as the defenders spawn close by, and mind your movement as the flag platform is separated by a deadly gap.
